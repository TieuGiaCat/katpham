Hi! I'm Kat

I'd like to tell you a little bit about myself. I consider myself extremely fortunate to be a graduate student majoring in Graphic design - visual communication.

After graduating, I began working as a UX/UI designer, which I really enjoy because it allows me to interact with people from a variety of professions. Then I had the opportunity to work as a project implementor, and in this position I can see a much broader picture than I could as a UX/UI designer.

And one thing that makes me feel very fortunate is that I have been taught many things by my seniors, as well as many valuable lessons to help me improve myself every day.

And this website is where I want to share my experiences as well as my knowledge about this creative industry!

As a UX/UI designer, I always try to see and feel the experiences around me more deeply, with the desire to learn to become a product designer to bring a better user experience and the most suitable solution for my clients.
