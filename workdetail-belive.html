BeLive Technology

BeLive is a technology company based in Singapore, where I took the position of Ux/Ui design and also as a visual designer. Through the opportunity to work at BeLive, I had learned a lot from how to communicate and work with the team, especially 100% of the team are foreigners, so that I have the opportunity to interact with different cultures and learn how to work in a global working environment. BeLive also gave me many opportunities to challenge myself in a new field and gave me the opportunity to learn a lot from my wonderful colleagues.

Ux/Ui products

Since BeLive is a start-up by nature, things grow very fast and BeLive products are improved every day, so I had the opportunity to join the product development team as a Ux/ui position. Design to develop new features. BeLive features aim to build a live streaming and e-commerce platform where live stream viewers can make live purchases quickly and sellers can track sales, purchases as well as interactions very easily.

Visual Design

Besides the position of Ux/Ui designer, I actively take on the position of Ux/ui designer because I really want to contribute more to the company in this field. Working in the position of Visual designer gave me the opportunity to work with the marketing team from which I learned more about the skills of designing media publications for the Singapore market.

One of my favorite products is the product promotion video for BeLive

As a UX/UI designer, I always try to see and feel the experiences around me more deeply, with the desire to learn to become a product designer to bring a better user experience and the most suitable solution for my clients.
