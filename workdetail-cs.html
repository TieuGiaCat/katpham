Cambridge Scout

Cambridge Scout is a Cambridge teaching center in Vietnam for secondary and high school students. Cambridge Scout will lay a solid foundation for students planning to study abroad in the United Kingdom, the United States, or Singapore, etc. Cambridge Scout's Branding requirement is to convey the message of student focus, collaboration, excellent, and trustworthy to students and parents.

Logo
