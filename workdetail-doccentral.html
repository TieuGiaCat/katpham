Doc Central

Medical app

Welcome to Doc Central, your one-stop solution for hassle-free medical appointments and online medical advice from trusted doctors in clinics across Singapore. In today's fast-paced world, understanding the value of time, and that's why this app is here to revolutionize your healthcare experience. With Doc Central, booking a medical appointment is as easy as a few taps on your smartphone. No more waiting on hold or standing in long queues at the hospital. Our user-friendly interface ensures that you can effortlessly find the right doctor, select a convenient time slot, and secure your appointment in no time. Each doctor in our platform undergoes a rigorous verification process, ensuring that you receive expert advice from qualified professionals.

But that's not all! Doc Central goes beyond appointment booking. Through Doc Central's integrated telemedicine feature, you can connect with doctors online, getting the medical advice you need from the comfort of your home or office. No more unnecessary travel or leaving work early for a simple consultation.

The Research

As the UX designer for Doc Central, my main focus is ensuring the best user experience. I actively conduct market and customer research, collaborating closely with the Singapore team despite working remotely. I gather insights from local contacts and potential users to understand their needs and preferences. With this knowledge, I make user-centric recommendations to improve the app and create a seamless experience for all Doc Central users. Collaboration, empathy, and user understanding drive our design process as we shape the future of healthcare accessibility in Singapore.

As a UX/UI designer, I always try to see and feel the experiences around me more deeply, with the desire to learn to become a product designer to bring a better user experience and the most suitable solution for my clients.

The Flow

After completing market and user research, my next step as a UX designer is to craft a comprehensive user flow. This will effectively communicate the desired user experience to the UI and development teams, outlining the entire app journey, interactions, and decision points. The user flow will incorporate insights from research and prioritize user preferences, aligning with our vision for a seamless and delightful experience on Doc Central. Through collaborative efforts, we'll refine the flow, iterate on the design, and create a go-to platform for convenient healthcare services in Singapore.

The System

As the UX designer, I've developed a comprehensive user flow and wireframe for Doc Central's admin system. This ensures both front-end and back-end users have a seamless experience while managing patient, clinic and doctor accounts. The user flow will show step-by-step navigation and helps identify potential pain points for optimization. The wireframe that I create also provides an intuitive layout for account management, considering all user needs. Collaboration with the development team and stakeholder feedback will refine the admin system for an efficient and user-centric experience on Doc Central.
