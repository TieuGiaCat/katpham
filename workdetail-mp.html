TC Consulting, LLC

One day, I was reached out and invited to collaborate on a Fintech product that the project makes for the US market to help people could be able to spill the bills and make payments become easier.

I have learned a lot and gained knowledge from my boss. Knowledge of the design is not limited to the design only, but also includes the knowledge around daily life and knowledge of the market.

Besides that, I learned how to work remotely with a company in a foreign country with time zone differences and different cultures, and I also learned how to communicate with people of different languages.

Professionally, I also learned how to develop products based on client ideas and requests, which I think is very important for Ux / Ui designers.

I really appreciate this opportunity

As a UX/UI designer, I always try to see and feel the experiences around me more deeply, with the desire to learn to become a product designer to bring a better user experience and the most suitable solution for my clients.
