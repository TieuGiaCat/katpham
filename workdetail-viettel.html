My Viettel

Viettel

My Viettel is my first real project after I graduate and I am very lucky to have been a part of this project. Viettel is one of the largest telecommunications companies in Vietnam. In 2021, Viettel changed its branding to be more modern and you can see the new logo and the new color on their website. This also means that their mobile app was also redesigned under the new branding. Fortunately, the agency I was working for is the agency that was chosen to implement this project.

My team is quite small with 3 members - I and two other one is the main designer for this product. We read all documents about Viettel's new branding and re-design the mobile app how to fit with the new spirit. Besides that, we don't have much time and the workload is very huge for a team of three members but after that, I learned a lot of things. Viettel is a big client so that they also have a lot of stringent conditions so that I have also trained myself to be a perfectionist with even with the smallest detail.

Working with the team also taught me how to work effectively and how to communicate with other members. The huge workload taught me how to manage my time to make sure the deadline is met on time

As a UX/UI designer, I always try to see and feel the experiences around me more deeply, with the desire to learn to become a product designer to bring a better user experience and the most suitable solution for my clients.
